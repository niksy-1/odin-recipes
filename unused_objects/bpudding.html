    • Bread Pudding

    Bread pudding is a pudding made with bread

    Ingredients needed

    1. 2 Slices of bread
    2. 3 Eggs
    3. 2 cups of milk
    4. Vanilla essence
    5. 1 cup sugar

    Optional ingredients

    Orange zest

    This is not necessary, but it gives your pudding a very tangy orange flavour
